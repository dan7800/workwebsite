Biography

I am a lecturer at the Rochester Institute of Technology, Department of Software Engineering. My research interests include Android permissions, code clone detection, and software engineering education research. I am particularly interested in how Android applications and malware can be further understood through the reverse engineering process. I spent more than seven years in industry working as a software engineer for several companies before transitioning to academia in 2010.
