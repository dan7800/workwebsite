Student Advising

My goal is to include undergraduate and graduate students in my research whenever possible. If you are a student looking to work with me on a research project, please e-mail me.

My expectations are:

  • Bi-weekly meetings.
  • An end of term report, which may or may not be directed towards a specific conference.
  • Regular deliverables which may include code, documentation, analysis, or writing.
  • Your Best Effort: Research is difficult. It is typically a long and difficult process where results are not immediate. Be prepared to do your best work, and put in the necessary time to achieve quality results.
